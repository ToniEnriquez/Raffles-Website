Our VISION STATEMENT

To uphold and advance the highest standards of aviation training for our Pilots.

Our Mission Statement

Provide safe and effective aviation training for our pilots. Inculcate professional discipline through academic experience to boost practical skills enhanced by an intensive yet friendly learning environment.

Our Founder: Mr Peh Teck Keng

TK Peh started his aviation career with the Republic Singapore Air Force where he served for over 36 years flying an array of fighter jets as a Full Colonel and was one of the legendary Black Knights.

He retired after serving several command and senior management positions to enter the commercial aviation industry specializing in developing flight training businesses.

As President of ST Aerospace Academy, he started training centers in Singapore, Australia, and the USA. The company pioneered the multi-crew pilot license (MPL) for a myriad of Singapore based airlines as well as for Qatar Airways. Currently, as the Managing Director of Raffles Aviation International, he provides aviation training business consultancy to multiple airlines worldwide, as well as training for them.

PROGRAM

Training duration is approximately 18 Months

PRIVATE PILOT
LICENCE

This course serves to provide the required aviation knowledge and flight training to candidates who want to obtain a PPL. Candidates will be taught on PPL ground theory subjects and achieve flight proficiency on a single-engine aircraft.

Duration:
Theory (90 hours)
Practical (40-50hours) - Single Engine

Course Structure:

  • PPL Ground Theory
  • Communication
  • Airlaw
  • Principle of Flight
  • Aircraft Systems
  • Flight Planning & Navigation
  • Meteorology

Entry Requirements:
Min A level or Poly Diploma
Pass Class 2 medical
